UR WIC Mission Statement

Our mission is to bridge the gender gap in computer science at the University of Rochester. This website is dedicated to our efforts in recognizing and supporting women in computing, both at our institution and all others around the world.

Read about the BRAID Initiave

The BRAID initiative was launched in September 2014 in partnership with universities across the nation to increase the percentage of women and students of color majoring in computer science. Each university has committed to a set of approaches to increase diversity within their computer science departments.
